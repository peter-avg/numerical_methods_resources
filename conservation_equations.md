## Conservation of Mass

- $\frac{Dp}{Dt} + \rho \nabla \cdot \mathbf{v} = 0$
- For incompressible flow: $\nabla \cdot \mathbf{v} = 0$
- Material form: $\frac{Dp}{Dt} + \rho \nabla \cdot v = 0$
- Spatial description: $\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot (\rho v) = 0$
- Material description: $\frac{Dp}{Dt} + \rho \nabla \cdot v = 0$

## Motion and Force Equilibrium

- $\rho \frac{\partial^2 \mathbf{u}}{\partial t^2} = f + \nabla \cdot \sigma$
- $\rho \frac{\partial \mathbf{v}}{\partial t} = f + \nabla \cdot \sigma$
- Zero body force $\Rightarrow f = 0$
- Stress distribution in equilibrium: $\nabla \cdot \sigma = 0$

## Conservation of Energy

- $\frac{D_pC_pT}{Dt} = \nabla \cdot k \nabla T + A + \sigma \colon D$
    - 1. Change in temperature with time
    - 2. Heat Transfer by conduction (and radiation)
    - 3. Heat Production (including latent heat)
    - 4. Heat Generated by internal deformation
- 1D Advection-Diffusion: $k \frac{\partial^2 T}{\partial^2 z} + v_z \frac{\partial T}{\partial z} = 0$

## Conservation of Momentum

- $\sigma_{ij} = \lambda \epsilon_{kk} \delta_{ij} + 2 \mu \epsilon_{ij}$
- For principal:

```math
\begin{bmatrix}
\sigma_1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & \sigma_2 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & \sigma_3
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
\lambda (\epsilon_1 + \epsilon_2 + \epsilon_3) + 2 \mu \epsilon_1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & \lambda (\epsilon_1 + \epsilon_2 + \epsilon_3) + 2 \mu \epsilon_2 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & \lambda (\epsilon_1 + \epsilon_2 + \epsilon_3) + 2 \mu \epsilon_3
\end{bmatrix}
```

- $-p = \frac{\sigma_{kk}}{3} = K \epsilon_{kk} = K \theta$
    - isotropic stress (i.e. pressure p)
- Deviatoric Stress: $\sigma_{ij}^{'} = \sigma_{ij} + p \delta_{ij} = 2G \epsilon_{ij}^{'}$
- Deviatoric Strain: $\epsilon_{ij}^{'} = \epsilon_{ij} - \frac{\theta}{3} \delta_{ij}$

## Navier-Stokes Equation

- Hydrostatic: $\nabla p = f$
- Isotropic Hookean solid: $\sigma_{ij} = \lambda \epsilon_{kk} I + 2 \mu \epsilon_{ij}$
    - Young's Modulus: $E = \sigma_{11} / \epsilon_{11}$
    - Poisson's Ratio: $v = - \epsilon_{33} / \epsilon_{11}$
- Isotropic Newtonian fluid: $\sigma_{ij} = (-p + \zeta D_{kk}) I + 2 \eta D_{ij}$
    - Newtonian Fluid: $\sigma_{ij}^{'} = \zeta D_{kk} \delta_{ij} + 2 \eta D_{ij}$
    - $\Delta = \nabla \cdot v = D_{kk}$
    - Force balance: $\nabla \cdot \sigma + f = \rho \frac{Dv}{Dt}$
    - Assuming constant yields for $\eta$ and $\zeta$: $-\nabla p + (\zeta + \eta) \nabla \Delta + \eta \nabla^2 v + f = \rho (\frac{\partial v}{\partial t} + v \cdot \nabla v) = \rho \frac{Dv}{Dt}$
- Incompressible Newtonian Fluid: $-\nabla p + \eta \nabla ^2 v + f = \rho (\frac{\partial v}{\partial t} + v \cdot \nabla v)$
