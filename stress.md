## Stress Transform to new Orthonormal Basis

- $\underline{\underline{\sigma_{'}}} = A \cdot \underline{\underline{\sigma}} \cdot A^T$
- $\sigma_{ij} = e_{i}^{'} \cdot \sigma_{ij} \cdot e_{j}$

## Stress Invariants

- $I_1 = \sigma_{kk}$
- $I_2 = \text{minor}(\underline{\underline{\sigma}}) = \sigma_{11}\sigma_{22} + \sigma_{22}\sigma_{33} + \sigma_{11}\sigma_{33} - \sigma^2_{12} - \sigma^2_{23} - \sigma^2_{31}$
- $I_3 = \text{det}(\sigma)$

## Deviatoric Stress

- $\sigma_{ij}^{'} = \sigma_{ij} - \frac{1}{3} \sigma_{kk} \delta_{ij}$
